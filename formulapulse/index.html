Free features

Pulse — F1 News Feed

Headlines from the top F1 outlets in one feed. Filter by driver, team, or source. "For You" carousel surfaces stories about your favorites first.

Full Season Schedule

Every race weekend on the calendar. Upcoming rounds with session times in your local timezone. Completed races show the P1–P3 podium at a glance.

Driver & Constructor Standings

Live championship tables for both standings. Tap any driver for a full profile — career stats, current season results, and teammate comparison.

Live Timing (Beta)

Real-time session data during practice, qualifying, and race. Positions, gaps, and sector times as they happen on track.

Pro features Subscription

Race Replay

Re-live every Grand Prix after the checkered flag. Full telemetry-based replay with position changes, gaps, and the timing board exactly as it happened.

AI Article Summary

One tap and any story is summarized instantly. Get the key points without reading the whole article — useful when you're catching up between sessions.

Save & Read Offline

Bookmark any story to your Library and read it later, even without a connection. Good for travel days and spotty paddock WiFi.

Full Past Race Details

Complete results and circuit information for every race in the season archive — not just the podium, every classified finisher.

No Ads

Clean reading throughout the app. No native ads in the news feed.

Apple Watch App

F1 news headlines and the session schedule on your wrist. Glanceable and synced with your iPhone.
