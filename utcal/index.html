UTCal

Live on App Store

A calibration tool for ultrasonic and PAUT inspectors. Built by a UT tech who got tired of doing the math on a notepad.

Download on the App Store

What it does

The math, handled

Thickness, velocity, and wedge delay calculations. Put in your measurements, get your result. No formula lookup, no notepad required.

Built for the field

Works offline, no account required. Your calibration data stays on your device — never on a server you don't control.

From a tech who's been there

UTCal was built by a practicing PAUT inspector. The workflow matches how UT actually works, not how someone imagined it might.
